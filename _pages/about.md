I am an Associate Professor at the [Department of Architecture and Architectural Engineering, College of Engineering, Seoul National University (SNU DAAE)](https://architecture.snu.ac.kr/). My research interests include spatial analysis, environmental behavior, and urban design, with research questions on the environment-behavior interactions. As long-term practical goals, I would develop future urban forms shaping healthy urban living. 

Before joining SNU DAAE, I worked at the [College of Architecture, Myongji University](http://arch.mju.ac.kr/) and [School of Architecture and Planning, University at Buffalo (SUNY)](http://ap.buffalo.edu) as a professor and at [Urban Form Lab](http://depts.washington.edu/ufl/) in University of Washington as a research associate. I also worked as an urban designer/researcher at Seoul Development Institute (currently [the Seoul Institute](http://www.si.re.kr/)).
